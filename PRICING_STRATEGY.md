## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: AI Hallucination Concerns
**Mitigation:**
- Clear disclaimers: "AI-assisted advice, not legal/financial advice"
- Source citations for regulatory guidance
- "Confidence score" on responses
- Professional liability insurance

### Risk 2: Low Conversion Rates
**Mitigation:**
- A/B test pricing ($79, $99, $119)
- Offer quarterly plans (lower commitment)
- Team discounts (buy 3, get 1 free)
- Money-back guarantee (30 days)

### Risk 3: Regional Payment Challenges
**Mitigation:**
- Multiple payment processors per region
- Local currency pricing (no FX fees)
- Alternative payment methods (e-wallets, bank transfers)
- Annual plans (one-time payment easier)

### Risk 4: Competitor Response
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage (build brand fast)
- Network effects (more users = better model)
- Lock-in via integrations (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Enterprise contracts (2-3 year terms)
